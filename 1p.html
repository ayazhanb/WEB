Dominican Republic is the best place to visit!

The Dominican Republicis a country located on the island of Hispaniola in the Greater Antilles archipelago of the Caribbean region.The Dominican Republic is the most visited destination in the Caribbean.A geographically diverse nation, the Dominican Republic is home to both the Caribbean's tallest mountain peak, Pico Duarte, and the Caribbean's largest lake and point of lowest elevation, Lake Enriquillo.

The Dominican Republic is often abbreviated as the Dominican Republic, which adds confusion to unclouded tourist heads, because in the same Caribbean there is the state of Dominica. Dominican Republic and Dominica are two different countries! Both countries have a visa-free travel regime for citizens of the Russian Federation, so pronounce the name of the desired route to the tour operator as clearly as possible.
