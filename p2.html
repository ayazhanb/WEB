Chicken pie

  • Time: 35 mins
Ingredients Quantity
Butter 210 gr
Flour 500 gr
Salt 1 tea spoon
Кefir 175 gr
Protein 1

Steps: Grind butter at room temperature with flour. Redeem soda in kefir. Add all the ingredients and knead the dough. Wrap the dough in foil and refrigerate for 30 minutes.

Flour 180 gr
Almond flour 65 g
Butter 120 gr
Yolk 1

Cake Oreo

  • Time: 30 mins
Ingredients Quantity
Sugar 300 gr
Flour 300 gr
Soda 1 tea spoon
Cocoa 50 gr
Eggs 4

Steps: Beat eggs and sugar until white. Add cream and sunflower oil. Stir until smooth. Add dry mixture. Divide in two and bake at 160 degrees for 30 minutes.
